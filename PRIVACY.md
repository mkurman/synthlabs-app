# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: January 27, 2026**

SynthLabs is an open-source project. We believe in privacy and simplicity.

## 1. Data Collection
We **do not** collect, store, or process any personal data from our users. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not track your activity on our site.

## 2. Cookies
We **do not** use cookies or any similar tracking technologies. Your browser storage remains yours.

## 3. Advertising
We **do not** serve ads. We have no interest in targeting users or building advertising profiles.

## 4. Third-Party Services
Since this is an open-source project, you can inspect our source code to verify that no hidden tracking or data-gathering mechanisms are present.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
If we ever change how we handle data, we will update this document and the source code accordingly.
